HELLO

Welcome to my website! I am Yuchin Choo currently enrolled in SAIC. Mainly work in the graphic design area. On this website, you will see the works that I produced in the 2022 academic year. Not super fancy, but some works will catch your eyes and inspire you. ENJOY!

Profile

I was born in Seoul, South Korea. I was a girl who loves everything. Loves playing sports, musical instruments, drawing, learning a second language, and even math. I studied everything that I love. When I was 17 years old, I decided on my pathway to graphic design. Reason? I can't remember, but one thing for sure is that I fell in love with the visual communication design exhibition at Chicago cultural center in 2018.

In 2020, I was accepted into SAIC. I majored in the Visual communication department. Making books, posters, and website UI/UX, I am learning lots of techniques in SAIC until now.

What I Make

I mainly work with digital applications, such as Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator. Not only these, but I also work with 3D objects or video editing. I have experience with Letterpress and Bookmaking as well.

Exploring diverse designs is my way of working. There are thousands of design categories in the world, and I enjoy experiencing them finding my new road is what I am doing right now.
